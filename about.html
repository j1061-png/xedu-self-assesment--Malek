About Xedu

Helping students find their path

We combine AI-powered guidance with structured self-reflection so students can understand their profile and take meaningful next steps.

Our mission

University applications are competitive and confusing. Xedu exists to give every secondary school student access to personalised, honest feedback about their academic and extracurricular profile — without the cost or wait of traditional advising.

What we believe

  • Every student deserves clear, actionable guidance
  • Self-reflection is the first step to growth
  • Technology should augment — not replace — human mentorship
  • Progress should feel motivating, not overwhelming

The self-assessment tool

Our free tool guides students through a conversation with an AI advisor, a structured self-assessment form, and a personalised report covering strengths, gaps, and a concrete next step. Students earn XP as they progress, making reflection feel rewarding.
